Custom Switch using Images

Customize:

the switch button in the off position with input.switch:empty ~ .switch-bar:after {}.

the switch button icon in the off position with input.switch:empty ~ .switch-bar .switch-icon.button-icon {}.

the switch bar in the off position with input.switch:empty ~ .switch-bar:before {}.

the switch bar icon in the off position with input.switch:empty ~ .switch-bar .switch-icon-off {}.

the switch button in the on position with input.switch:checked ~ .switch-bar:after {}.

the switch button icon in the on position with input.switch:checked ~ .switch-bar .switch-icon.button-icon.

the switch bar in the on position with input.switch:checked ~ .switch-bar:before {}.

the switch bar icon in the on position with input.switch:checked ~ .switch-bar .switch-icon-on {}.
