Alerts with Embedded Links

Well done! You just read the alert message successfully.
Heads up! This alert needs your attention.
Warning! This alert is a warning message.
Be Careful! Something is not right.

</div>

Dismissible Alerts

Heads up! This alert needs your attention.
Warning! This alert is a warning message.
Be Careful! Something is not right.

  </button>
  ...
</div>
