Tabular Notification

Align content inside a notification like a table with .tabular-notification.

Use a combination of .notification-field and .notification-content to control the size of each cell. .notification-field has width: 1% and will expand only as wide as its content as long as .notification-content is present as a sibling.

.notification-content will expand to fill the remaining space.
