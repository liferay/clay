Figure (Image Captions)

Add captions to your images with .figure and .figcaption or use HTML5 elements ```

figcaption

figcaption-bottom
Carajillo strong, grounds redeye half and half so percolator.
figcaption-top
Carajillo strong, grounds redeye half and half so percolator.
figcaption-full

Figures with Crop Img

Combine figure with crop-img to add captions to your cropped images, setting the height on figure or crop-img is required.

figcaption
Carajillo strong, grounds redeye half and half so percolator.
figcaption-bottom
Carajillo strong, grounds redeye half and half so percolator.
figcaption-top
Carajillo strong, grounds redeye half and half so percolator.
