Collapsible List Group

Create collapsible list groups with List Group and the Collapse module.

Add collapse-icon to list-group-heading to display a toggle icon between collapsed and open states. The icons can be changed by editing the $list-group-header-icon-closed and $list-group-header-icon-open variables.
